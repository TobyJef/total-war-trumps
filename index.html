How To Win At Total War: Trumps

Take on the mantle of Shogun and plan your attack. Choose your warrior but pick wisely, as a rival Daimyo is challenging your position for Supreme Ruler of Japan. Choose one of your finest Archers, Samurai, Yari Spearmen or Cavalry, to fight for glory against your dishonourable foe. Archers rain down arrows on Samurai from afar. Samurai wield their swords up close against Yari Spearmen. Use Yari Spearmen to hold off charging Cavalry. Close the gap against far away Archers with a Cavalry attack. Can you defend your Kingdom?
