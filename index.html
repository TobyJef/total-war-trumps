How To Win At Total War: Trumps

Take on the mantle of Shogun and plan your attack. A rival Daimyo is challenging your position as Supreme Ruler of Japan.

Choose either your finest Archer, Samurai, Yari Spearmen or Cavalry. To fight for glory against this dishonourable foe.

Archers rain down arrows onto Samurai from distance. The Samurai wield their katanas up close against Yari Spearmen.

Use Yari Spearmen to hold off charging Cavalry. Or close the gap against far away Archers with a Cavalry attack.

Best your rival to maintain your dynasty or face the ultimate punishment.

In the event of a stalemate, no points are awarded

The first player to 5 Victories will win the campaign.

Can you defend your Kingdom?

Remember:

Archers defeats Samurai. Samurai defeats Spearmen. Spearmen defeats Cavalry. Cavalry defeats Archers.

Achers stalemate Spearmen. Samurai stalemate Cavalry.

First player to 5 Victories wins the campaign.
